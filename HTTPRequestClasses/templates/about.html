About Master Patient ID:

Master Patient ID is a patient-matching software that has the potential to become fully implemented and used within a healthcare enterprise. The product uses both the patient’s demographics and other important patient data within their Continuity of Care Document (CCD) file to match with other records in the system to identify the patient. Currently, we are using HL7 FHIR and the SIIM Hackathon Server to create and test MPI. One day, this product can give clinicians a standard way to share a comprehensive yet concise representation of a patient’s medical history and current condition.
